Annotation:
    - An annotation is extra information associated with a particular point in a document or other piece of information. [wiki]
        It can be a note that includes a comment or explanation. 
        Annotations are sometimes presented in the margin of book pages.

Bookmark:
    - In the context of the World Wide Web, a bookmark is a Uniform Resource Identifier that is stored for later retrieval in any of various storage formats. [wiki]
        All modern web browsers include bookmark features.
    - 1: a marker for finding a place in a book. 
        2 : a menu entry or icon on a computer that is usually created by the user and 
            that serves as a shortcut to a previously viewed location (such as an Internet site) bookmark
